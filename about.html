Janos Fulop is a self-taught artist resides in Hungary. Working with a lot of different kind of materials and technics. For a while he worked with stainless steel making "one of a kind" jewelry pieces. Recently he picked up oil painting again and getting more and more into digital art at the same time. He has a passion for large scale art either on canvas or digital. His pieces are meant for large empty walls to fill. For prints inquiries please contact via the form on the contact page.
